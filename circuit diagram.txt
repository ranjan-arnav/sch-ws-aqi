

Circuit Diagram:
DHT22 Sensor:

VCC to 5V
GND to GND
Data to Digital Pin 2
BMP280 Sensor:

VCC to 3.3V (or 5V, check sensor specifications)
GND to GND
SCL to A5 (SCL)
SDA to A4 (SDA)
MQ-135 Sensor:

VCC to 5V
GND to GND
AOUT to A0
LCD Display (with I2C):

VCC to 5V
GND to GND
SDA to A4 (SDA)
SCL to A5 (SCL)
SD Card Module (Optional):

VCC to 5V
GND to GND
CS to Pin 10
MOSI to Pin 11
MISO to Pin 12
SCK to Pin 13
